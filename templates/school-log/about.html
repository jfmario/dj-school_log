School Log

This application is designed to help you log schooling hours for students. In the Students section, manage your students. Note that deleting a student will delete all entries associated to it. In the Subjects section, you can add or remove subjects. Manage your entries in the Entries section, where you can also query a subset of your entries for export to HTML, CSV, or PDF.

For any comments, feedback, or questions, submit a Help Ticket or create an issue at the GitHub repository.
